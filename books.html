Containers and Docker Compose for Self-Hosting

How Containers and Docker Compose can Make Self-Hosting Fun and Easy for Everyone!

Break free from the cost and shrinking value of traditional online services and replace them with ones that you can run on computers you already own. Worry not, you do not have to be a seasoned system's administrator to take advantage of these technologies. Self-Hosting is a fun hobby for everyone. Containers and Docker Compose make it simple to get started, while also providing remarkable flexibility the more your needs and hobby grows. It's no secret that the world of online services is getting more expensive and less useful by the day. Practically every service and utility online require a monthly subscription or trades cost for your personal information, of which will be sold or used to influence your life and decisions you make.

Self-Hosting empowers you to break away from these negative and harmful socio-techno-patterns. Containers and Docker Compose make running services in your home quick and easy, removing the traditionally heavy burden of trading time and effort in exchange of money, so that you can get back to your weekend and not toil away fighting numerous technical issues when you just want to watch a movie that you already own, on your TV.

Includes over 50 practical examples to get you up and running with containers and docker compose.
